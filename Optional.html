Bob Ross Ipsum

We start with a vision in our heart, and we put it on canvas. Maybe there's a happy little waterfall happening over here. We'll lay all these little funky little things in there. I started painting as a hobby when I was little. I didn't know I had any talent. I believe talent is just a pursued interest. Anybody can do what I do. You can work and carry-on and put lots of little happy things in here. No pressure. Just relax and watch it happen.
